Welcome to Aperture Science! Get ready to defy physics and solve mind-bending puzzles with the Portal Gun in Portal 2, the sequel to the award-winning Portal. Play in the single-player story as Chell, a test subject who awakens in a deserted enrichment center after a lengthy slumber. Navigate through the facility using high-tech portals you create with the Portal Gun, and come face-to-face with the rogue AI GLaDOS.
